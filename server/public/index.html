For SLT, mobile apps are becoming prevalent in clinical practice. Mobile apps can support in the clinical management by supplementing speech therapy, increasing practice time, offering biofeedback or information relating to the accuracy of the user’s attempt, enhancing families’ engagement with speech therapy, and by strengthening therapists’ ties with clients by offering them extended treatment throughout the day.
